Hounsfield HU

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Hounsfield HU does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

What the app does

Hounsfield HU is an on-device, educational medical-imaging assistant. It runs an AI model (MedGemma) entirely on your iPhone to help you explore chest X-ray and CT images. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnoses.

Data collection: none

The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, identifiers)
  • Medical images or reports you create in the app
  • Usage data, crash logs, or device information
  • Location data

Where your data lives

All studies, images, clinical context, and AI-generated reports are stored only in the app's private storage on your device, encrypted at rest with AES-GCM. If you enable the biometric lock, access is additionally gated behind Face ID or Touch ID; biometric data itself is handled entirely by iOS and never leaves the Secure Enclave — the app never sees it.

Network access

The AI model ships inside the app, and inference runs entirely on-device. The app works fully offline, including in airplane mode. The only network activity the app can perform is one you explicitly initiate — for example, choosing to download an alternative model. No image, report, or personal data is ever transmitted.

Sharing and export

If you choose to export or share a study using the iOS share sheet, the data goes only to the destination you pick. The app itself sends nothing anywhere.

Camera and photo library

The app requests camera and photo library access solely so you can capture or import an image of a radiograph for on-device analysis. Those images stay in the app's encrypted local storage.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for medical education and is not directed at children. Since no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Because the app collects nothing, we expect changes to be rare.
